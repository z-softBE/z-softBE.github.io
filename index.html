    About Us

    Z-Soft always tries to help its customers by really listening what they want and figure out together what the best solution is. Below you can read more on how we try to achieve this:

    We listen

    We start with listening to what your ideas are and what you want. After that we will start asking questions about features, we will do some suggestions and create a dialog so you can get the product you want that is best suited for you.

    We develop

    When everything is agreed upon we start with the development. We will set a deadline for the first version. During the development we will ask you feedback of things that are already impelmented. This makes sure that the feedback loop is short and we can act quickly when there is something you want to have changed.

    Support

    When everything is implemented and you are statisfied with the result. We either give you your website/application and explain everything you need to know about your product. Or we can do the hosting for you and we start putting everything online. After we delivered the product we are still available to fix issues or answer questions you may have. Supporting our clients is really important for us!
